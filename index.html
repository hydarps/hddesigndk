About Us

We’re a small, local hobby business from Kongerslev Denmark driven by a passion for creating high-quality, custom-designed products at affordable prices. Our mission is to provide unique and beautifully crafted items without compromising on quality, and we proudly use eco-friendly wood fiber in our creations.

At HD design.dk, we believe that great design doesn’t have to come with a hefty price tag. Every piece we create is a labor of love, meticulously designed to meet your needs and preferences. Whether you’re looking for a special gift or something to brighten up your space, our custom designs are made to fit your vision and budget.

Thank you for supporting our small business. We’re excited to share our creations with you and look forward to helping you find something truly special!
